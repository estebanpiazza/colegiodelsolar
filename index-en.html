Who we are

Over 40 years educating with purpose

We are an institution recognized for our commitment to integral, innovative and student-centered education.

We were founded with a clear vision: preparing students for life. Today we sustain that commitment by integrating new methodologies, technology and pedagogy, while keeping our core identity.

We combine tradition and innovation with a deeply human perspective. We shape curious, critical and creative students ready to thrive in a changing world.

Excellence Academic, emotional and human development at every stage
Innovation In the classroom and connected to real life
Community Strong bonds between students, families and school
"Education goes beyond content delivery: it means developing people with the tools they need for life."

Educational proposal

A continuous educational journey with purpose

A continuous pathway from 45 days old to high school, articulating each stage with educational coherence and progressive growth.

Early Childhood

Learning through play, bonding and exploration

From 45 days old, we build a caring, sensitive and trustworthy environment where children discover, explore and learn through playful experiences.

  • Innovative proposal grounded in neuroscience
  • Support for biopsychosocial needs in early childhood
  • Early English acquisition and development of the mother tongue
  • Teaching team in continuous professional development
Primary School

Strengthening learning with autonomy and critical thinking

In Primary School, we offer an innovative bilingual proposal where students are protagonists of broad, diverse and meaningful learning experiences.

  • Neuroeducation, multiple intelligences and emotional education
  • Opportunities and challenges that strengthen skills and abilities
  • Teacher facilitators focused on student-centered learning
  • Inclusive, supportive and empathetic environment
High School

University orientation and future readiness

High School offers bilingual education within a Natural Sciences-oriented program, with university preparation, international outlook and social responsibility.

  • Six years organized into Basic Cycle and Oriented Upper Cycle
  • Innovation, critical thinking and social responsibility
  • Cambridge, Perugia and Italian as a second foreign language
  • Global College USA agreement for academic and sports opportunities
  • Olympiads, Model UN, international trips, camps and festivals

Family participation

Parents' Civil Association of Colegios del Solar

A network of families committed to school life and to building an active, welcoming and purposeful educational community.

The Parents' Civil Association of Colegios del Solar is made up of families committed to institutional life and to the development of the educational community.

Its purpose is to strengthen the bond between families and the school by promoting collaborative work, active participation and spaces that foster wellbeing, inclusion and a sense of belonging.

What does the Parents Committee do?

The Committee promotes initiatives that enrich the school experience of students and their families, supporting the growth of the educational community.

  • Organizing activities that encourage connection and integration.
  • Supporting institutional projects.
  • Creating spaces for dialogue and participation between families and the school.

A community that builds together

Through its work, the Committee seeks to build a united, participatory and committed community around an education that goes beyond the classroom and creates social impact.

The Association invites all families to join and take an active role in this project, convinced that the joint work between families and school strengthens the integral development of students.

Family + School: united to drive actions that create change.

Bilingual education

Bilingual learning with international standards

As a Cambridge Partner, we deliver a learning journey aligned with international standards of English-teaching excellence.

We understand language as a tool for communication, thinking and global access. We foster multicultural awareness so students can perform naturally in international contexts.
